# AWS — Certification Tips

## Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02) — Week 4
- Entry-level, builds confidence
- Easy if you've been doing hands-on labs
- Resume checkbox for Canadian employers

## Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) — Week 8

### Study Strategy
```
Week 5-6: Watch course videos (2 hrs/day alongside Docker/Terraform)
Week 7:   Practice exams (Tutorials Dojo — do ALL 6 sets)
Week 8:   Review weak areas, take exam

Passing score: 720/1000
Question format: multiple choice + multiple select
Time: 130 minutes, ~65 questions
```
