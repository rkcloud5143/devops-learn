# Interview Prep — Behavioral Questions (QA → DevOps Story)

Frame answers using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

## "Tell me about yourself"
> "I have 6 years of experience in software quality assurance where I
> ensured application reliability through testing. I've now transitioned
> to DevOps because I want to ensure reliability at the infrastructure
> level. I've earned AWS Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect
> certifications, and I've built production-grade infrastructure using
> Terraform, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines. My QA background gives me a
> unique perspective — I think about failure modes and edge cases that
> others might miss."

## "Why DevOps?"
> "In QA, I was always the last line of defense before production. I
> realized I wanted to be involved earlier — building the infrastructure
> and pipelines that prevent issues from reaching production in the
> first place. DevOps lets me apply my quality mindset to the entire
> delivery pipeline."
